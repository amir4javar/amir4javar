## What I Work On

My work sits at the intersection of **NLP/ML research** and **applied AI engineering**. I'm especially drawn to problems where model efficiency meets real-world utility:

- **Self-Distillation** — Compared Born-Again Networks and layer-wise self-distillation (BYOT) against standard training on CIFAR-100. BAN achieved +1.4% accuracy over baseline through iterative self-teaching across generations. [[repo]](https://github.com/amir4javar/born_again_networks)
- **Multi-Teacher Distillation** — Investigated whether combining soft labels from multiple teachers improves distillation on GLUE benchmarks. Published a negative result: teacher diversity, not quantity, is the key bottleneck. [[repo]](https://github.com/amir4javar/multi_teacher_distillation_experiment)
- **Retrieval-Augmented Generation** — Built a production-ready multi-turn RAG system with hybrid search (vector + BM25), a 4-node LangGraph reasoning pipeline, and real-time streaming over WebSockets. [[repo]](https://github.com/amir4javar/CompArch)
